## Template Categories

**Product Releases** - Monitor when companies announce release dates

**Price Tracking** - Track price changes and get alerts when items drop below thresholds

**Stock Availability** - Check when out-of-stock items become available

**Event Tickets** - Monitor when tickets go on sale

## Customizing Templates

Templates provide starting points with placeholders for:
- Product names
- Price thresholds
- Store/retailer names
- Locations
- Dates/timeframes

After selecting a template, customize these placeholders to match what you want to monitor. The schedule and notification behavior are pre-configured but can be adjusted.
